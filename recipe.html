Ingredients for Making Jalebi

  • All purpose flour / Maida - 100 gm

  • Curd / Yoghurt - 1 cup

  • Lime juice - 1 tsp

  • Cornstarch / Cornflour- 30 gm

  • Hot oil - 1 tbsp

  • saffron color/food colouring-1 tsp

  • Sugar - 1 cup

  • Water - Â½ cup

  • Oil for deep frying

How to Make Jalebi at Home

  1. Mix flour, cornflour, lime juice, curd, food colouring till it forms a thick batter.

  2. Heat oil and pour hot oil over this and mix well. Let this ferment overnight.

  3. Heat sugar and water in a sauce pan for 5 mins till it gets transparent and syrupy consistency.

  4. Heat oil for deep frying.

  5. Now take the jalebi batter and spoon it in the piping bag and pipe it in hot oil.

  6. Fry it till crisp and light golden. Put this in hot sugar syrup and let it sit for 30 sec.

  7. Take it out and serve hot with some milk.
